Water is life!

Background

"Watelikers," an advocacy initiative, has been established to address pressing challenges related to water safety and drinkability worldwide. Comprising passionate individuals, experts, and c oncerned citizens, Watelikers aims to transform the water industry, promoting a healthier environment and enhancing the well-being of global inhabitants. Through collaborative efforts and effective measures, Watelikers strives to ensure sustainable access to clean and safe drinking water for everyone on Earth.

Actions

Watelikers coordinate clean-up events, workshops, infrastructure upgrades, and policy advocacy to ensure universal access to clean drinking water. Their multifaceted approach addresses pressing water safety challenges, striving for a world where safe drinking water is a fundamental right for all.
